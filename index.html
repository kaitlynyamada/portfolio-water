Case Study 001

Water U Doing?

Feel the thrill of competition while conquering your hydration goals.

Staying hydrated is an extremely aspect to maintain a person's health. Unfortunately many people forget to or are unmotivated to drink water throughout the day.

With this in mind, my team and I took the initiative and designed the "Water U Doing?" mobile app to addess this issue.My involvements include: conducting semi-structured interviews, designing low fidelity prototypes, user testing, transforming low fidelity prototypes to high fideltiy prototypes, and adding UI interactions.

Project Description:

Timeline: December 5, 2022 to January 10, 2023 (approximately 1 month)
Role: UX/UI Designer
Collaborators: Annie Baker & Zoe Jonas

Overview

THE PROBLEM

Did you know that 75% of people are chronically dehydrated?

THE SOLUTION

In order to address this, my team and I designed a mobile application that help users drink more water through creating a community of health conscious individuals to better be able to reach their hydration goals.
